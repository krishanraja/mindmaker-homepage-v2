## Anti-Outcomes (What We DON'T Promise)

**We DON'T promise:**
- ❌ Technical AI skills or tool proficiency
- ❌ Custom AI strategy deliverables (we provide frameworks, not consulting)
- ❌ Overnight transformation (literacy compounds over time)
- ❌ Consensus on every AI decision (we surface tensions, not eliminate them)
- ❌ Guaranteed ROI on AI pilots (we improve decision quality, not execution)

## Success Metrics (How We Measure)

### For Individual Leaders:
- **Self-Reported Confidence:** Pre/post diagnostic confidence scores
- **Decision Velocity:** Time to make AI-related decisions (tracked quarterly)
- **Risk Avoidance:** Number of bad AI bets avoided (self-reported)
- **Behavioral Indicators:** Frequency of challenging vendor claims in meetings

### For Executive Teams:
- **Alignment Score:** Pre/post team diagnostic alignment metrics
- **Decision Cycle Time:** Avg time to green-light AI initiatives (before/after)
- **Pilot Success Rate:** % of AI pilots that achieve stated goals
- **Cost Savings:** Estimated savings from avoided bad bets

### For Investment Firms:
- **Portfolio Coverage:** % of portfolio companies benchmarked
- **Risk Identification:** Number of high-risk companies flagged early
- **CEO Satisfaction:** Portfolio CEO feedback on value-add
- **Pilot Failure Reduction:** % decrease in failed AI pilots across portfolio
