# User Outcomes

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-25

---

## Outcome Framework

Success = tangible, measurable outcomes (not completion metrics). Users pay for results.

---

## Individual Leader Outcomes

### After Initial Consult (45 min)
**Immediate:**
- Clarity on AI applicability to specific problem
- Understanding of engagement options
- Confidence to make program decision

**Measurable:**
- 90%+ understand what's possible
- 70%+ proceed to paid program within 30 days

---

### After Builder Session (60 min)
**Immediate:**
- 1 AI friction map
- 1-2 draft systems ready to test
- Written follow-up with prompts

**30 Days Later:**
- 50%+ deployed at least 1 system
- 2-5 hours/week saved
- 40%+ proceed to Sprint

**Measurable:** Time saved, quality improvement, confidence score

---

### After Builder Sprint (30 days)
**Immediate:**
- 3-5 working systems deployed
- Builder Dossier
- 90-day roadmap

**90 Days Later:**
- 80%+ still using systems
- 8-15 hours/week saved
- 2-3 additional systems built independently

**6 Months Later:**
- 60%+ built 5+ additional systems
- 10-20x ROI
- 70%+ feel AI-literate

**Measurable:** Systems count, time saved, ROI, team trained, confidence

---

## Team Outcomes

### After Leadership Lab (Half/Full Day)
**Immediate:**
- Shared language/framework
- 2 decisions processed with AI
- 90-day pilot charter

**90 Days Later:**
- Pilot complete with documented results
- ROI measured
- Decision on scaling

**Measurable:** Systems deployed, adoption rate, time/cost saved, decision quality

---

## Portfolio Partner Outcomes

### After 6-12 Month Engagement
**Portfolio-Wide:**
- Complete maturity assessment
- Prioritization matrix
- 10-20 companies engaged
- Reusable frameworks

**Per Company:**
- 1-5 systems deployed
- Documented use cases/ROI

**Partner Firm:**
- Differentiated value prop
- Case studies
- Enhanced brand

**Measurable:** Companies engaged, systems deployed, frameworks created, renewal rate

---

## Outcome Categories

### 1. Time Savings
- Session: 2-5 hrs/week
- Sprint: 8-15 hrs/week
- Lab: 5-10 hrs/week per person

### 2. Quality Improvement
- 30-50% error reduction
- 2-3x faster analysis
- Better decision confidence

### 3. Capability Building
- Confidence: 3/10 → 8/10
- 5-10 systems within 6 months
- 3-5 team members trained

### 4. Business Impact
- 10-20x ROI within 6 months
- $50K-$200K annual value (individual)
- $500K-$2M annual value (team)

---

## Anti-Outcomes

❌ Not: "AI transformation"  
✅ Instead: Specific systems solving specific problems

❌ Not: "AI strategy"  
✅ Instead: Working implementations with measurable results
