# Ideal Customer Profile

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-25

## Primary ICPs

### ICP 1: Senior Operating Leader
**Titles:** CEO, COO, GM, CCO, CPO, CMO, CRO  
**Characteristics:** 10+ years leadership, P&L accountability, $500K+ budget authority, 20-500 person teams  
**Pain Points:** Drowning in AI vendor pitches, pressure from board, skeptical of theory-only training  
**Success Metrics:** 1-3 working systems deployed within 30 days, confidence to evaluate vendors

### ICP 2: Executive Team
**Profile:** Series B to late-stage startup, $10M-$100M revenue, 50-500 employees  
**Decision Makers:** CEO + C-suite (6-12 executives)  
**Pain Points:** Fragmented AI efforts, no shared language, pilots that go nowhere  
**Success Metrics:** 90-day pilot charter, 2+ decisions run through AI, measurable ROI

### ICP 3: Portfolio Partner
**Profile:** VC firm (20-100 portfolio cos), advisory firm (10-50 clients)  
**Decision Makers:** Managing Partner, Operating Partner  
**Pain Points:** Can't scale 1:1 advisory, need repeatable frameworks  
**Success Metrics:** Portfolio assessment, 10-20 companies engaged, demonstrated value-add

## Anti-ICPs
❌ Technical teams ❌ Early-stage founders ❌ Individual contributors ❌ Government/highly regulated

**End of ICP**
